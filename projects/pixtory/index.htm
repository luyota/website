Pixtory gives you a connection to your surroundings that you didn't have before. Its driving philosophy is simplicity: just tell it to start...and that's it.

While running in the background it will only ever bother you with an easily recognizeable vibration when it finds pictures, simply as reassurance that it's got you covered, or maybe a suggestion that, if you've got a moment, there's something nearby that's worth photographing yourself.

When your travels are done Pixtory gives you a photographic narrative of your journey, which is yours to curate, admire, and share.

It gives you your travel stories, in pictures, effortlessly.
